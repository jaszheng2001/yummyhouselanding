ABOUT Yummy House

High Quality Chinese and Thai Cuisine

Yummy House is located in Flushing, NY proudly offering high quality chinese food since 2005. We're committed to provide excellent food and service in our community. Conveniently located one block from Flushing Hospital, come in and grab a quick bite. Or you can give us a call for free delivery for orders over $7, other restrictions may apply. In addition, we also provide catering service.


OUR STAFF

Our staffs are exceptionally trained with multiple years of experience and well certified in many aspects, including food protection. They're passionate about what they do and work to ensure our customer's satisfaction.

OUR FOOD

Our ingredients are sourced locally to ensure freshness of our food. Our relationship with our vendors allow us to source our ingredients at lower cost without sacrificing on quality. Additionally, we're able to cater to your taste bud on most cuisines.

CATERING

Do you have a big event or gathering comming up? Whether the event is a Halloween party, birthday party, or an event, we can provide for them. Contact us for more details to learn more about what we offer!
